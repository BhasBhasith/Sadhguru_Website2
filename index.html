Our Vision & Mission

Our vision is to bring our customers' dreams to life by providing tailored services and cost-effective materials, supported by in-house vehicles and machinery. We are committed to continuous improvement, always listening to our clients to deliver better solutions every day, and ultimately becoming the number one choice in the minds of our customers and partners.

Our mission is to provide top-quality construction, technical, and management services, building long-term relationships with clients based on safety, quality, and timely delivery. We prioritize fair treatment of employees and continuous improvement to ensure cost-effective, responsive service.

Who We Are

Established two decades ago, Gurubabaji Group of Companies is one of the leading government road contractors, private layout developers, and building material suppliers in and around Coimbatore, The Nilgiris, Erode, and Tirupur districts. We have completed numerous BT works for Highways, DRDA, Municipalities, Town Panchayats, Village Panchayats, and private layouts. We are also one of the leading suppliers of cement, building blocks, metal, sand, pipes, and other essential construction materials. Furthermore, we specialize in subcontracting and managing government tenders for buildings, roads, villas, and commercial establishments. With a proven track record in healthcare and commercial projects across South Tamil Nadu, the company is renowned for excellence. Our recent expansion into Sathguru Babaji Earth Movers has further enhanced our offerings with value-added services at affordable prices for our customers and partners.
